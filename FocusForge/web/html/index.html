Features

Everything you need to
crush exam season

From tracking your first exam to downloading your final revision schedule, FocusForge has you covered.

Exam Tracker

Add all your upcoming exams with dates, difficulty levels, and reminders. See what's coming at a glance and never miss a deadline.

AI Study Plan

Set your study hours, session length, and availability window. The AI generates a day-by-day schedule prioritised by exam proximity and difficulty.

Email Reminders

Get notified 1 day, 3 days, or 1 week before each exam. Reminders are sent automatically via SendGrid — set it and forget it.

5 Languages

Full UI translation in English, Spanish, French, Greek, and Italian. Switch instantly from the settings menu on any page.

Secure Auth

Username/password login with SHA-256 hashing, rate-limiting, and Google OAuth via Firebase — your data stays yours.

Dark Mode

Full dark theme that respects your system preference. Easy on the eyes during those late-night study sessions.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Create a free account

Sign up with a username and password, or continue with Google. No credit card, no nonsense.

2

Add your upcoming exams

Enter the subject, date, difficulty, and reminder preference for each exam. Takes about 10 seconds per exam.

3

Generate your AI study plan

Set your daily study hours and availability window. FocusForge builds a full schedule, prioritising the subjects that need the most attention first.

4

Study, track, and ace it

Follow the plan, get your reminders, mark exams as completed, and record your grades. Your plan is saved across devices.

Tech Stack

Built with real tools,
not tutorials

FocusForge is built from scratch in C++ — no Node, no Python, no hosted backend services. Everything runs on metal.

⚡ C++ (Crow framework)
🗄️ SQLite
🔐 OpenSSL (SHA-256)
🔥 Firebase Auth (Google OAuth)
📧 SendGrid Email API
🤖 Claude AI (study plans)
🌍 i18n (5 languages)
🎨 Vanilla JS + CSS
📦 vcpkg + CMake
